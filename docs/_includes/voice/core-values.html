Core values

Simplify

Weniger, aber besser (Dieter Rams)

Simplifying is not about sacrificing, it’s about becoming more intentional. It’s about making space for the things that truly matter to you.

Consume less, create more

بالشعب و للشعب Bil-shaʿb wa lil-shaʿb

Not about living in the past, not about rejecting the future, it’s about being in the present.

Why make? Because it feels good!

Consumption: not just buying behaviour, but your information diet, what you allow into your home and life. Consuming less leads to simplicity.

Less, but better

Weniger, aber besser (Dieter Rams)

Strive for simplicity: remove the excess and eliminate the things that don’t serve you so you can fully engage and enjoy.

Curate your life, create a home you love and surround yourself with only things you feel good about

Use your dollars to support people that act in alignment with your values.

Support local makers, ecopreneurs and people that are participating in creating a positive world.

Progress, not perfection

There’s a crack in everything. That’s how the light gets in. (Leonard Cohen)

Perfection is not the goal - that’s a distraction

It’s potentially damaging to your health and wellness

Be comfortable with progress

Progress means you’re growing as a person

Never want to present myself as someone who has achieved a green life - I’m also on a journey
