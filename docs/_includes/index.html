Little Green Dot Style Guide

The Little Green Dot Style Guide is at the heart of our drive to bring consistency to our product UI and efficiency to our development process.

Based on the Bootstrap framework, it is a well-defined collection of components that can be used to assemble new UIs quickly without accruing new technical debt. It is also a place for anyone to understand the dos and don'ts of working with Little Green Dot's brand.

How it benefits us

  • We can present Little Green Dot's brand and voice consistently across all channels
  • We can focus on workflows and logic, not buttons and list items
  • We can build consistently and quickly without needing design oversight for every pixel
  • We can maintain our UI code in one place and keep redundancy to a minimum

Our style guide is a living reference tool for developers, designers, marketers and product managers. We share it here in the hope that it might help others on a similar path.
